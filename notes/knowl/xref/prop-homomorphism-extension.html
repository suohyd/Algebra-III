Proposition 32. Extension of homomorphism.

Suppose that \(F\) is a field and that \(\overline{F}\) is an algebraic closure of \(F\text{.}\) If \(K/F\) is an algebraic field extension and an embedding \(i_K\colon K\hookrightarrow\overline{F}\) which is the identity on \(F\text{.}\) If \(L/K\) is an algebraic field extension, then there exists an embedding \(i_L\colon L\hookrightarrow\overline{F}\) which extends \(i_K\) and is the identity on \(F\text{.}\)
